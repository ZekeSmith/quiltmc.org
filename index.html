The Quilt Project

The Quilt project is designed around three main goals: speed, ease of use, and modularity. These three pillars of Quilt combine to create a slick and modern modding toolchain and ecosystem.

This is Quilt

Posted by Haven King & ToffeeMax on 20 April 2021

This is the Quilt Project: An open, community-driven modding toolchain designed primarily for Minecraft. Quilt builds on many of the technologies developed by the Fabric Project and is designed to take what worked well and expand on it further.
